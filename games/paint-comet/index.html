Paint Comet - How to Play

Control a glowing comet that paints beautiful trails as it moves through space!

Controls

Click or tap anywhere to guide your comet. The comet uses physics-based movement - click far away for more force, close for gentle movement. Upward clicks give extra boost!

Objective

Hit the gold glowing walls to spawn stars! Each wall hit creates 3 new stars in random positions. Collect stars by passing through them. No goal - just paint and collect!

Mechanics

The comet leaves a beautiful glowing trail wherever it goes. Hit the walls (4 on each side) to spawn stars. The trail fades over time, creating dynamic, artistic patterns!

Strategy

Use the physics movement to bounce off walls and create stars. The comet's momentum carries it, so plan your path to hit walls and collect the stars they spawn!
